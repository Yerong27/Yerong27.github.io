Love is not fair on its own

Love is not fair on its own

Like the dark side of the Moon

The scale tilts to the side

Where hearts with heavy desire reside

Countless plays abide by one rule

One soul on fire —

Till it cools

A burning gaze —

Rests upon a nameless bloom

Scorched —

Descending into the tomb
